# Tempo Strategic Roadmaps - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Overview

Tempo Strategic Roadmaps (formerly Roadmunk) offers tiered pricing for individuals, small teams, and enterprises. Pricing shifted toward enterprise focus after Tempo's acquisition in 2021.

## Current Pricing Tiers (2025)

### Starter Plan
| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Price** | $19/month |
| **Users** | 1 Roadmapper |
| **Reviewers** | 3 included |
| **Roadmaps** | Unlimited |
| **Best For** | Solo product managers, individual use |

**Included Features**:
- Unlimited roadmaps
- Feedback collection
- Basic idea management
- Timeline and swimlane views
- PowerPoint export
- 3 reviewer accounts

### Business Plan
| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Price** | $49/editor/month |
| **Minimum** | 5 users ($245/month minimum) |
| **Reviewers** | Additional included |
| **Best For** | Small to mid-sized teams |

**Included Features**:
- Everything in Starter
- Portfolio roadmaps
- API access tokens
- Two-way integrations (Jira, Azure DevOps)
- Advanced collaboration
- Priority support

### Professional Plan
| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Price** | $99/editor/month |
| **Minimum** | Custom |
| **Best For** | Scaling teams with security needs |

**Included Features**:
- Everything in Business
- Single Sign-On (SSO/SAML)
- Customized training
- Dedicated support
- Advanced security controls

### Enterprise Plan
| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Price** | Custom (contact sales) |
| **Users** | Unlimited reviewers |
| **Best For** | Large organizations |

**Included Features**:
- Everything in Professional
- Private Cloud option
- Advanced customization
- Unlimited reviewers
- Custom contract terms
- SLA guarantees
- Enterprise security

## Pricing Examples

### Solo Product Manager
**Starter Plan**:
- 1 editor × $19 = $19/month
- Annual: ~$228/year

### Small Team (5 editors)
**Business Plan**:
- 5 × $49 = $245/month (minimum)
- Annual: $2,940

### Mid-Size Team (15 editors)
**Business Plan**:
- 15 × $49 = $735/month
- Annual: $8,820

### Enterprise Team (50+ editors)
**Professional/Enterprise**:
- Custom pricing required
- Volume discounts available
- Contact sales for quote

## Billing Options

- **Monthly**: Available for all plans
- **Annual**: Discounted (contact sales)
- **Enterprise**: Custom billing terms

## Upcoming Pricing Changes

**Effective September 15, 2025**:
- New pricing for Cloud and Data Center apps
- Applies to all Tempo brands (Tempo, Prime, ALM Works, Old Street Solutions, Alpha Serve)
- 60-day protection window for existing quotes

## Reviewer Seats

Tempo offers "Reviewer" seats for stakeholders who need view/comment access:
- **Starter**: 3 reviewers included
- **Business**: Additional reviewers
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited reviewers

This provides cost-effective stakeholder access without full editor licensing.

## Comparison to Competitors

| Tool | Starter | Team/Business | Enterprise |
|------|---------|---------------|------------|
| **Tempo Roadmaps** | $19/mo | $49/user/mo | Custom |
| **ProductPlan** | $39/mo | $69/user/mo | Custom |
| **Productboard** | $19/mo | $59/user/mo | Custom |
| **Aha!** | $59/mo | $99/user/mo | $149/mo |
| **Airfocus** | $59/mo | Custom | Custom |

### Value Analysis

**Tempo vs. ProductPlan**:
- Tempo cheaper at Business tier ($49 vs $69)
- Tempo requires 5-user minimum
- Tempo has deeper Jira integration

**Tempo vs. Productboard**:
- Similar entry pricing
- Tempo stronger on roadmap visualization
- Productboard stronger on feedback management

**Tempo vs. Aha!**:
- Tempo significantly cheaper at all tiers
- Aha! more comprehensive feature set
- Tempo simpler, faster to implement

## Post-Acquisition Pricing Impact

Since Tempo acquired Roadmunk (November 2021):
- Prices increased
- Minimum user requirements added
- Enterprise focus intensified
- Original SMB-friendly positioning shifted
