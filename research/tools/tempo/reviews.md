## Capterra Review Analysis

### Detailed Pros (User Quotes)

#### Ease of Use
> "Easy to use - I didn't even need training and could figure it all out by myself."

> "Roadmunk is a great tool that allows you to easily create and share roadmaps and collect and organize feedback. It integrates perfectly well with JIRA and has a simple, effective and intuitive user interface."

#### Multiple Visualization Options
> "Having all the options they have makes it easy to manage a single roadmap and share different views with different stakeholders."

> "It delivers multiple presentation views—like timeline, swimlane, and table—from a single dataset, empowering teams to present strategic plans clearly and quickly."

#### Visual Quality
> "Effortless, functional and could produce professional looking roadmaps."

> "Saved literally weeks worth of time."

#### Jira Integration
> "Collaboration is seamless: it integrates with tools like Jira, Azure DevOps, and more."

#### Customer Support
> "The onboarding/customer support is great with lots of resources on the web."

### Detailed Cons (User Quotes)

#### Pricing Concerns
> "The price of utilizing Roadmunk can be a restricting aspect for small businesses or startups."

> "Prices jumped. Minimum user requirements appeared. The focus shifted to enterprise. That accessible roadmapping tool for growing teams? It's now part of an enterprise suite with enterprise pricing to match."

#### Customization Complexity
> "While Roadmunk offers extensive customization options, it can sometimes feel overwhelming to navigate through the numerous settings and configurations. Simplifying the user interface or providing more guided tutorials for beginners would be beneficial."

#### Integration Limitations
> "Its integration with other systems is rather poor, which means I must manually update things constantly and thus the tool isn't saving me any time at all."

#### Reliability Issues
> "I experienced a number of instances when it was not working. This started to become more and more annoying as I was using this to manage my clients as a consultant."

#### Mobile Experience
> "While you can access and view roadmaps on mobile devices, the functionality and editing abilities are limited. This is often a constraint for end users who often work on the go."

#### Feature Limitations
> "I would have liked to apply custom weights to the prioritization aspect of the roadmap, but this is not available at the lower price-point."

> "Easy to use, but overall we were not able to use this as our sole roadmap solution."
