# Tempo Strategic Roadmaps - Users, Personas & Use Cases

## Target Users

### Primary Personas

#### 1. Product Managers
**Role**: Day-to-day roadmap creation and management

**Daily Activities**:
- Create and update product roadmaps
- Collect and process customer feedback
- Prioritize features using RICE/Value vs. Effort
- Communicate plans to stakeholders
- Sync roadmaps with Jira/Azure DevOps

**Why They Choose Tempo**:
- Beautiful, professional roadmap visualizations
- Easy drag-and-drop interface
- Multiple views from single dataset
- Stakeholder-friendly sharing options

#### 2. Product Directors/Leaders
**Role**: Portfolio oversight and strategic alignment

**Daily Activities**:
- Review portfolio-level roadmaps
- Ensure cross-team alignment
- Present to executives
- Manage dependencies across products
- Track strategic initiatives

**Why They Choose Tempo**:
- Portfolio roll-up capabilities
- Executive-ready presentations
- Cross-project visibility
- PowerPoint export for board meetings

#### 3. Project Managers
**Role**: Project planning and coordination

**Daily Activities**:
- Plan project timelines
- Track deliverables and milestones
- Coordinate across teams
- Report progress to stakeholders
- Manage dependencies

**Why They Choose Tempo**:
- Timeline visualization
- Jira integration for execution tracking
- Resource alignment with Capacity Planner
- Clear milestone tracking

#### 4. Agile Teams & Scrum Masters
**Role**: Sprint and release planning

**Daily Activities**:
- Plan sprints and releases
- Connect roadmap to Jira backlog
- Communicate priorities to team
- Track velocity and progress
- Manage stakeholder expectations

**Why They Choose Tempo**:
- Deep Jira integration
- Real-time sync with sprints
- Integration with Tempo Timesheets
- Capacity planning alignment

## Use Case Examples

### Use Case 1: Jira-Connected Product Roadmapping

**Scenario**: Product team needs roadmaps that sync with Jira development work

**Workflow**:
1. Connect Strategic Roadmaps to Jira
2. Configure field mappings and sync options
3. Import epics/stories into roadmap
4. Visualize development work on timeline
5. Set up bi-directional sync
6. Track Jira status from roadmap view
7. Share progress with stakeholders

**Value**: "The integration with Jira is seamless and keeps our roadmap aligned with what the team is actually building."

### Use Case 2: Portfolio Visibility Across Products

**Scenario**: Product Director needs to see all product roadmaps in one view

**Workflow**:
1. Create individual roadmaps per product
2. Build portfolio roll-up roadmap
3. Configure views by team, theme, or timeline
4. Identify cross-product dependencies
5. Share portfolio view with executives
6. Present at quarterly business reviews

**Value**: "Finally can see how all our products fit together and identify conflicts before they happen."

### Use Case 3: Customer-Driven Prioritization

**Scenario**: PM needs to prioritize features based on customer feedback

**Workflow**:
1. Set up Feedback Portal with unique URL
2. Share portal with customers/stakeholders
3. Collect feedback submissions
4. Link feedback to ideas in Idea Manager
5. Score ideas using RICE framework
6. System ranks by priority score
7. Move top ideas to roadmap

**Value**: "Having a direct link between what customers ask for and what we build has transformed our prioritization."

### Use Case 4: Stakeholder Communication

**Scenario**: Share roadmap with different audiences without recreating

**Workflow**:
1. Build master roadmap with all details
2. Create executive view (high-level themes)
3. Create team view (detailed items)
4. Create customer view (public commitments)
5. Share appropriate view per audience
6. All views update from single data source

**Value**: "Love that I can create different views for different people without managing multiple spreadsheets."

### Use Case 5: Release Planning with Capacity

**Scenario**: Align release roadmap with team capacity

**Workflow**:
1. Build roadmap in Strategic Roadmaps
2. Connect to Tempo Capacity Planner
3. View team availability against planned work
4. Adjust timeline based on capacity
5. Sync to Jira for sprint planning
6. Track actual vs. planned with Timesheets

**Value**: "The Tempo suite together gives us a complete picture from strategy to execution to tracking."
