# ChatPRD - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Overview

ChatPRD offers simple, accessible pricing designed for individual PMs to enterprise teams. The pricing is notably lower than traditional PM tools, reflecting its focused scope on documentation rather than full PM platforms.

### Value Analysis

**ChatPRD vs. Traditional PM Tools**:
- ChatPRD is 4-7x cheaper than Aha!, Productboard, Airfocus
- Different focus: Documentation vs. Full PM platform
- Complementary rather than replacement for most teams

**ChatPRD vs. Generic LLMs**:
- ChatPRD: $8-15/month, purpose-built for PRDs
- ChatGPT Plus: $20/month, general purpose
- Claude Pro: $20/month, general purpose
- ChatPRD advantage: PM-specific training and templates

**ChatPRD vs. Free Alternatives**:
- Free alternatives exist (FreePRD.ai, Write My PRD free tier)
- ChatPRD offers more advanced features, customization
- Team collaboration requires paid ChatPRD plans

## Value Proposition Summary

ChatPRD is positioned as an accessible, high-ROI tool:
- Low cost relative to alternatives
- High time savings
- Focused on specific pain point (documentation)
- Complements rather than replaces PM tools

At $8-24/user/month, it's priced to be an easy "yes" for most PMs and teams compared to the time savings it provides.
