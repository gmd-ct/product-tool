## Feature Gaps & Limitations

### Known Limitations
- Learning curve steep for new users
- Can be overwhelming for small teams
- Non-Jira integrations less robust
- GitLab integration formatting issues
- Feature hierarchy can be complex
- AI tools noted as "rigid" by some users

### Missing/Weak Areas
- Native time tracking
- Built-in capacity planning
- Advanced workflow automation
- Deep analytics/BI capabilities
