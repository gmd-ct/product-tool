# Productboard - Users, Personas & Use Cases

## Target Users

### Primary Personas

#### 1. Product Managers
**Role**: Core platform users who manage the product lifecycle

**Daily Activities**:
- Collect and organize customer feedback
- Prioritize features based on customer impact
- Build and update product roadmaps
- Communicate plans to stakeholders
- Link insights to strategic objectives

**Why They Choose Productboard**:
- Single source of truth for customer insights
- Data-driven prioritization
- Seamless feedback-to-roadmap workflow
- Strong Jira integration for dev handoff

#### 2. Product Leaders / Directors / VPs
**Role**: Strategic oversight and portfolio management

**Daily Activities**:
- Review cross-product insights
- Ensure alignment with company objectives
- Report to executives on product strategy
- Manage product team resources

**Why They Choose Productboard**:
- Pulse for portfolio-level insights
- OKR alignment features
- Executive-ready roadmap views
- Cross-team visibility

#### 3. Customer Success Managers
**Role**: Contributors who capture customer voice

**Daily Activities**:
- Log customer feedback from calls
- Tag feature requests to accounts
- Track customer-requested features
- Communicate roadmap updates to customers

**Why They Choose Productboard**:
- Easy feedback submission
- Customer-to-feature linking
- Account-based insights
- Portal for customer communication

#### 4. Sales Teams
**Role**: Contributors who capture prospect needs

**Daily Activities**:
- Log feature requests from prospects
- Link deals to feature requests
- Track competitive gaps
- Communicate roadmap to prospects

**Why They Choose Productboard**:
- Salesforce integration
- Opportunity value tracking
- Easy contribution workflow
- Prospect-specific roadmaps

#### 5. Support Teams
**Role**: Contributors who capture user issues

**Daily Activities**:
- Forward support tickets as feedback
- Tag recurring issues
- Track feature request volume
- Close the loop with customers

**Why They Choose Productboard**:
- Zendesk/Intercom integration
- Easy ticket-to-insight workflow
- Automatic categorization
- Customer communication

## Use Case Examples

### Use Case 1: Customer-Centric Prioritization

**Scenario**: PM needs to prioritize Q2 features based on customer demand

**Workflow**:
1. Feedback collected from Intercom, Zendesk, Salesforce
2. AI (Pulse) auto-categorizes and identifies trends
3. Features ranked by customer impact score
4. Salesforce opportunity values added
5. Prioritization matrix used for final decisions
6. Top features added to roadmap

**Value**: "Pull feedback from sales, support, and customers all in one spot and tie it directly to features. Makes prioritization much more aware."

### Use Case 2: Cross-Functional Alignment

**Scenario**: Product team needs to align engineering, sales, and leadership

**Workflow**:
1. Product team creates internal roadmap
2. Customer-facing roadmap created for portal
3. OKRs linked to features
4. Jira sync configured for engineering
5. Sales team views roadmap in Salesforce context
6. Leadership views Pulse for portfolio insights

**Value**: "The tool is great as a single source of truth of user insights, becoming the go-to place for the Product team."

### Use Case 3: Feedback Loop Closure

**Scenario**: Close the loop with customers who requested features

**Workflow**:
1. Customer submits feature request via portal
2. PM links to existing feature or creates new
3. Feature prioritized and added to roadmap
4. Status updated as development progresses
5. Customer automatically notified on release
6. Changelog published to portal

**Value**: "Ability to seamlessly integrate user feedback directly into the product development process."

### Use Case 4: Enterprise Portfolio Management

**Scenario**: Product leader managing multiple product lines

**Workflow**:
1. Each product has dedicated Productboard workspace
2. Pulse provides cross-portfolio insights
3. Themes identify company-wide trends
4. Leadership dashboard shows strategic alignment
5. Resource allocation informed by data

**Value**: "Productboard is a great platform for providing visibility into roadmaps across different product areas."

### Use Case 5: Sales-Product Alignment

**Scenario**: Close deals faster by addressing prospect needs

**Workflow**:
1. Sales logs feature requests in Salesforce
2. Requests sync to Productboard with opportunity value
3. High-value requests bubble up in prioritization
4. PM reviews sales-driven demand
5. Roadmap updated with sales-influenced features
6. Sales can share roadmap with prospects

**Value**: "Connect customer feedback to features and prioritize based on real customer insights."

## Team Structures That Work Well

### Customer-Centric Product Teams
- PM owns Productboard as source of truth
- CS, Sales, Support as contributors
- Engineering as consumers via Jira
- Leadership as viewers

### Cross-Functional Product Organization
- Multiple product teams in teamspaces
- Shared insights board
- Consolidated customer portal
- Portfolio-level Pulse views

### Enterprise Product Operations
- Central product ops team
- Standardized processes
- Template roadmaps
- Governance via roles/permissions
