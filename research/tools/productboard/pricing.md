# Productboard - Pricing Analysis

## Current Pricing Model (2025)

### Pricing Structure
- **Model**: Per maker, per month
- **Billing**: Monthly or Annual (annual saves ~20%)
- **Free Roles**: Contributors and Viewers unlimited on all plans
- **Free Trial**: Available

### Plan Tiers

| Plan | Price (Monthly) | Price (Annual) | Best For |
|------|-----------------|----------------|----------|
| **Starter** | Free | Free | Individuals, evaluation |
| **Essentials** | $25/maker/mo | $19/maker/mo | Small teams starting out |
| **Pro** | $75/maker/mo | $59/maker/mo | Growing product teams |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | Large organizations |

## Detailed Plan Comparison

### Starter (Free)
**Best For**: Individuals or very small teams evaluating the platform

**Included**:
- Basic feedback collection
- Simple roadmaps
- Release planning
- Feedback portal
- 20+ integrations
- Limited features (5 per product)
- 1 maker seat

**Limitations**:
- Feature caps
- Limited customization
- No advanced analytics
- Basic support

### Essentials ($19/maker/month annual)
**Best For**: Small product teams getting started

**Included**:
- Everything in Starter
- Unlimited features
- Multiple roadmap views
- Basic prioritization
- Jira integration
- Standard support

**Annual Cost Examples**:
- 1 maker: $228/year
- 5 makers: $1,140/year
- 10 makers: $2,280/year

### Pro ($59/maker/month annual)
**Best For**: Growing teams needing advanced capabilities

**Included**:
- Everything in Essentials
- Customer portal (public)
- Customer updates and communication
- Connect feedback to features
- Advanced prioritization
- Driver-based scoring
- Objectives and key results
- Advanced integrations
- Priority support

**Annual Cost Examples**:
- 1 maker: $708/year
- 5 makers: $3,540/year
- 10 makers: $7,080/year
- 25 makers: $17,700/year

### Enterprise (Custom Pricing)
**Best For**: Large organizations with security/compliance needs

**Included**:
- Everything in Pro
- SAML SSO
- Google SSO
- Advanced security features
- Audit logs
- Custom terms
- Dedicated support
- Implementation assistance
- Advanced analytics
- Priority access to new features

**Estimated Pricing**:
- 20 makers: $70,000-$100,000/year (~$300-400/maker/month)
- Varies significantly based on negotiation

## Maker vs. Contributor vs. Viewer

| Role | Access Level | Cost |
|------|--------------|------|
| **Maker** | Full edit, create, prioritize, manage | Paid per seat |
| **Contributor** | Add feedback, comment, collaborate | Free (unlimited) |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to roadmaps | Free (unlimited) |

### Maker Capabilities
- Edit and update features
- Create and manage roadmaps
- Prioritize feature ideas
- Configure integrations
- Manage settings (admin makers)
- Full platform access

### Contributor Capabilities
- Submit feedback and ideas
- Add comments
- View roadmaps
- Participate in discussions
- Cannot edit features or roadmaps

### Viewer Capabilities
- View shared roadmaps
- Read-only access
- Cannot comment or submit feedback

## Value Analysis

### Best Value For
- Teams needing strong feedback management
- Customer-centric product organizations
- Companies with many non-maker stakeholders (free contributors/viewers)
- Jira-centric development teams

### May Not Be Cost-Effective For
- Very small teams (1-2 people) - free tier limited
- Teams not focused on customer feedback
- Organizations needing deep customization
- Budget-constrained startups

## ROI Considerations
