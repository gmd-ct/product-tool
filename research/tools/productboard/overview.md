## Company History

Productboard was founded in 2014 by Daniel Hejl and Hubert Palan with the mission to help product teams build products that matter. The company emerged from the founders' experience in product management and their frustration with existing tools' inability to properly capture and organize customer feedback.

The company grew from a small Prague-based startup to a global unicorn, becoming one of the most well-funded product management platforms in the market.
