## Feature Gaps & Limitations

### Known Limitations
- Steep learning curve
- Complex initial setup
- Can be overwhelming for small teams
- Premium pricing
- Certification expensive (~$2,300)

### Not Ideal For
- Simple roadmap needs
- Budget-constrained startups
- Teams wanting minimal learning curve
- Basic feedback collection only
