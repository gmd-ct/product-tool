# Aha! - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Overview

Aha! uses modular pricing where each product is priced separately. Products can be used independently or combined for discounts.

## Combined Pricing Examples

### Small Product Team (5 users)

**Roadmaps Only**:
- 5 × $59 = $295/month
- Annual: $3,540

**Roadmaps + Develop**:
- 5 × ($59 + $9) = $340/month
- Annual: $4,080

### Mid-Size Team (15 users)

**Roadmaps Only**:
- 15 × $59 = $885/month
- Annual: $10,620

**Full Suite (Roadmaps + Ideas Advanced + Develop)**:
- 15 × ($59 + $20 + $9) = $1,320/month
- Annual: $15,840

### Enterprise Team (50 users)

**Enterprise Roadmaps + Ideas + Develop**:
- 50 × ($99 + $20 + $18) = $6,850/month
- Annual: $82,200

## Billing & Contract Terms

### Billing Options
- **Monthly**: Credit card only, higher pricing
- **Annual**: 20% discount, invoice option available

### Price Increases
- 3% annual increase standard
- Predictable cost escalation

### Minimum Users
- Aha! Ideas: Minimum 3 paid users
- Other products: 1 user minimum

### Free Trial
- 30 days for most products
- 14 days for select products
- Full feature access during trial

## Comparison to Competitors

| Tool | Entry Price | Mid-Tier | Enterprise |
|------|-------------|----------|------------|
| **Aha! Roadmaps** | $59/mo | $99/mo | $149/mo |
| **Productboard** | Free/$19/mo | $59/mo | Custom |
| **ProductPlan** | ~$39/mo | ~$69/mo | Custom |
| **Airfocus** | $19/mo | $69/mo | $89/mo |

### Aha! vs. Productboard Full Comparison

To use both roadmaps and feedback features:
- **Aha!**: $59 (Roadmaps) + $20 (Ideas Advanced) = $79/user/mo
- **Productboard Pro**: $59/user/mo

Aha! is ~33% more expensive for comparable functionality but includes more products.

## Hidden Costs & Considerations

### Additional Costs
- **Certification**: ~$2,300 for Aha! Roadmaps certification
- **Advanced Add-ons**: Ideas Advanced, Knowledge Advanced, Whiteboards Advanced
- **Enterprise Features**: SSO requires Enterprise tier
- **Training**: May need consulting for complex setups

### Cost Savings
- Products include Essentials of other products (e.g., Roadmaps includes Ideas Essentials)
- Bundle discounts for multiple products
- Annual billing saves ~20%

### Volume Discounts
- Available for larger teams
- Contact sales for enterprise pricing
- Multi-year discounts possible

## Value Analysis

### Best Value For
- Enterprise product organizations
- Multi-product portfolios
- Teams needing comprehensive suite
- Strategic product management focus
- Organizations valuing bootstrapped/stable vendor

### May Not Be Cost-Effective For
- Small startups (< 10 people)
- Simple roadmap-only needs
- Budget-constrained teams
- Teams preferring simplicity

## ROI Considerations

### Time Savings
- Integrated suite reduces tool switching
- AI assistant accelerates documentation
- Automated workflows reduce manual work

### Strategic Value
- Better prioritization through data
- Improved stakeholder alignment
- Clearer strategy-to-execution connection

### Total Cost of Ownership
- Higher upfront cost vs. alternatives
- Steeper learning curve = higher training investment
- Comprehensive features may reduce need for other tools
