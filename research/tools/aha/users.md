# Aha! - Users, Personas & Use Cases

## Target Users

### Primary Personas

#### 1. Senior Product Managers
**Role**: Strategic product planning and execution

**Daily Activities**:
- Define product strategy and vision
- Create and maintain roadmaps
- Prioritize features and initiatives
- Align teams with company objectives
- Report progress to leadership

**Why They Choose Aha!**:
- Comprehensive strategic planning tools
- Deep prioritization frameworks
- Professional roadmap presentations
- OKR and goal alignment

#### 2. Product Leaders / Directors / VPs / CPOs
**Role**: Portfolio oversight and strategic direction

**Daily Activities**:
- Manage multiple product lines
- Set organizational product strategy
- Allocate resources across teams
- Report to executives and board
- Ensure strategic alignment

**Why They Choose Aha!**:
- Portfolio management across products
- Executive-ready reporting
- Pivot tables and dashboards
- Enterprise-grade governance

#### 3. Product Operations Managers
**Role**: Process and tool management for product org

**Daily Activities**:
- Configure and maintain Aha! instance
- Standardize processes across teams
- Train product managers
- Generate reports and analytics
- Manage integrations

**Why They Choose Aha!**:
- Extensive customization options
- Standardized templates
- Multi-team management
- Advanced admin controls

#### 4. Engineering Leaders
**Role**: Development planning and execution

**Daily Activities**:
- View roadmap for planning
- Manage development sprints
- Track engineering capacity
- Report technical progress
- Coordinate with product

**Why They Choose Aha!**:
- Aha! Develop for engineering
- Deep Jira/Azure DevOps integration
- Roadmap-to-sprint alignment
- Capacity planning tools

## Use Case Examples

### Use Case 1: Enterprise Strategic Planning

**Scenario**: CPO needs to align 10 product teams with company OKRs

**Workflow**:
1. Define company-level goals in Aha!
2. Create initiatives that support goals
3. Each product team creates aligned roadmaps
4. Features linked to initiatives
5. Progress rolls up to executive dashboard
6. Quarterly reviews in pivot table reports

**Value**: "Connect goals and initiatives to actual work so teams can stay focused on delivering the most customer and business value."

### Use Case 2: Ideas Portal for Customer Feedback

**Scenario**: PM needs to collect and prioritize customer requests

**Workflow**:
1. Launch branded Ideas Portal
2. Customers submit and vote on ideas
3. Ideas linked to Salesforce opportunities
4. Revenue-based prioritization scoring
5. Best ideas promoted to roadmap
6. Customers notified of status changes

**Value**: "Crowdsource ideas from customers and employees, rank based on value, then promote the best ones directly to your roadmap."

### Use Case 3: PM-Engineering Alignment

**Scenario**: Reduce friction between product and engineering teams

**Workflow**:
1. PM creates roadmap in Aha! Roadmaps
2. Features pushed to Jira via two-way sync
3. Engineering works in Jira
4. Status updates flow back to Aha!
5. PM has visibility without context switching
6. Both teams see real-time progress

**Value**: "Seamless Jira integration saves time by eliminating the need for developers to switch between applications."

### Use Case 4: Customer Research with Discovery

**Scenario**: Research team needs to centralize customer interviews

**Workflow**:
1. Upload interview recordings to Discovery
2. AI transcribes in 100+ languages
3. AI identifies key takeaways
4. Create highlight clips
5. Link insights to roadmap items
6. Build evidence-based prioritization

**Value**: "Centralize meeting transcripts, uncover learnings, and tie insights directly to product plans."

### Use Case 5: Multi-Product Portfolio Management

**Scenario**: Product leader managing 5 product lines

**Workflow**:
1. Each product has dedicated workspace
2. Shared goals across portfolio
3. Portfolio dashboard shows all roadmaps
4. Pivot tables for cross-product analysis
5. Resource allocation visibility
6. Executive reporting from single view

**Value**: "See not only the detail view of any given project, but also the macro view across all projects in a rolled-up view."
