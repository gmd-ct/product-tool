## Company History

ProductPlan was launched in 2013 by Jim Semick with the mission to help product teams visualize, plan, and communicate their product strategies. Over the past 13 years, the company has grown from a focused roadmapping tool to a comprehensive Product Lifecycle Management platform.

## Mission & Vision

ProductPlan positions itself as "the easiest way to plan, visualize, and communicate your product strategy." The platform emphasizes:

- Strategic alignment across organizations
- Intuitive, visual roadmapping
- Single source of truth for product teams
- Enterprise-grade reliability with ease of use

## Product Suite Evolution

ProductPlan has evolved from a pure roadmapping tool to a comprehensive platform with modules for:

1. **Strategy** - Goal and objective alignment
2. **Prioritization** - Scoring and ranking initiatives
3. **Idea Capture** - Stakeholder feedback collection
4. **Roadmapping** - Visual planning and communication
5. **Product Launch** - Go-to-market coordination
6. **Product Intelligence** (New via Winware.ai) - AI-powered insights
