# ProductPlan - Pricing Analysis

## Current Pricing Model (2025)

### Custom/Quote-Based Pricing

As of July 2024, ProductPlan moved to **custom, enterprise pricing** with no public pricing displayed.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Pricing Model** | Custom quote-based |
| **Billing Cycle** | Annual only (no monthly option) |
| **User Type** | Per editor (viewers unlimited) |
| **Minimum Seats** | Required but unspecified |
| **Free Trial** | Available |

### How Pricing is Determined

ProductPlan calculates pricing based on:
- Organization size and team structure
- Targeted use cases
- Integration requirements
- Support level needed
- Contract length

### Getting a Quote

1. Book a 15-minute consultation
2. Discuss organizational needs
3. Receive tailored proposal

## Historical Pricing (Pre-July 2024)

Before pricing was hidden, ProductPlan had transparent tiers:

### Previous Tier Structure

| Plan | Price (per user/month) | Key Features |
|------|----------------------|--------------|
| **Basic/Business** | $39 | Core roadmapping |
| **Professional** | $89 | Advanced features |
| **Enterprise** | $69 | Team features |
| **Enterprise Plus** | $99 | Full platform |

### Historical Pricing Notes
- 5 editors on Basic plan: $2,940/year upfront
- Pricing started at $119/month for some configurations
- SSO required extra payment (now included)

## Included in All Plans

All ProductPlan plans now include:

### Core Features
- Unlimited roadmaps
- Unlimited viewers
- Customizable roadmap views
- Portfolio views
- Prioritization board
- Roadmap standardization

### Advanced Features
- Integrations (Jira, Azure DevOps, Slack, etc.)
- OKRs and objectives
- In-app notifications
- Unlimited teams
- Idea capture and discovery
- AI-suggested initiatives
- Launch management
- Risk and dependency tracking

### Admin & Security
- Single Sign-On (SSO)
- Advanced admin management

## Billing & Contract Terms

### Payment Options
- Credit card
- Wire transfer
- ACH

### Contract Terms
- Annual billing required
- Multi-year discounts available
- **Price Protection**: Renewal rates capped at maximum 5% annual increase

### Unique Value
> "Unlimited audience access, so everyone in your organization can view and collaborate without extra costs."

## Editor vs. Viewer Distinction

| User Type | Capabilities | Cost |
|-----------|--------------|------|
| **Editor** | Create roadmaps, edit items, use planning board, manage permissions | Paid per seat |
| **Viewer** | View roadmaps, add comments | Free (unlimited) |

## Comparison to Competitors

### ProductPlan vs. Alternatives

| Tool | Pricing Model | Starting Price |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| **ProductPlan** | Custom quote | ~$39-99/user/mo (historical) |
| **Productboard** | Tiered | Free - $80/maker/mo |
| **Aha!** | Tiered | $59-149/user/mo |
| **Airfocus** | Tiered | $19-89/user/mo |

### Pricing Perception

**Pros**:
- Unlimited viewers included
- All-inclusive feature set
- Price increase protection (5% cap)
- No surprise add-on costs

**Cons**:
- Lack of transparency (quote required)
- No monthly billing option
- Minimum seat requirements
- Perceived as expensive vs. alternatives
- SSO previously required extra payment

## Total Cost of Ownership Considerations

### Direct Costs
- Editor seat licenses
- Annual commitment

### Hidden/Additional Costs
- Implementation time (though noted as fast)
- Training (minimal due to ease of use)
- No add-ons for features (all-inclusive)

### Cost Savings
- No viewer seats to purchase
- Reduced tool sprawl (all-in-one platform)
- Fast onboarding reduces time-to-value

## Recommendations

### Best Value For
- Organizations with many stakeholders needing view access
- Teams prioritizing roadmap visualization
- Companies wanting predictable pricing (5% cap)

### May Not Be Cost-Effective For
- Very small teams (minimum seat requirements)
- Organizations needing month-to-month flexibility
- Teams requiring deep feedback management (may need additional tools)
