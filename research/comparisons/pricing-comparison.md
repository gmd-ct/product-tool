## Hidden Costs & Add-Ons

### ProductPlan
- No significant add-ons
- What you see is what you get

### Productboard
- **Reviewers**: Free (view-only)
- **Contributors**: Mid-tier pricing
- **Ideas Portal**: Included in Pro+
- **Implementation**: May need services

### Aha!
- **Ideas Portal**: +$39/user/mo
- **Develop**: +$9/user/mo
- **Whiteboards**: Included in some plans
- **Most comprehensive = most add-ons**

### Airfocus
- **Integrations**: Scale plan required
- **API Access**: Scale plan required
- **SSO**: Enterprise required

### Tempo
- **Jira Integration**: Business+ required
- **SSO**: Professional+ required
- **5-user minimum**: Business plan

### ChatPRD
- **Premium AI**: Pro+ required
- **Custom Templates**: Pro+ required
- **Team Features**: Team plan required

## Total Cost of Ownership Considerations

### Beyond Subscription
1. **Implementation Time**: More complex = higher cost
2. **Training**: Steeper learning curve = more training
3. **Integration Effort**: More integrations needed = more work
4. **Adoption Risk**: Complex tools may fail to adopt

### Estimated TCO Rankings (Lower = Better)

1. **ChatPRD** - Lowest (simple, focused)
2. **ProductPlan** - Low (simple roadmapping)
3. **Productboard** - Medium (more features, more setup)
4. **Airfocus** - Medium (modular, customizable)
5. **Tempo** - Medium-High (requires Jira ecosystem)
6. **Aha!** - High (most comprehensive, most complex)

## Recommendation by Budget

### Budget-Constrained (<$5k/year)
- **ChatPRD** for documentation
- **ProductPlan Basic** for roadmapping
- Consider free/open-source alternatives

### Mid-Budget ($5-20k/year)
- **Productboard Pro** for feedback-focused
- **ProductPlan Professional** for roadmapping
- **Airfocus Core** for modular approach

### Well-Funded ($20k+/year)
- **Aha!** for comprehensive needs
- **Productboard Scale** for feedback at scale
- **Tempo Enterprise** for Atlassian shops
